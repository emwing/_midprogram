Spring 2017 Digital Marketing Final

Independently research, develop, design, and present a cohesive digital marketing plan for the PacSet Tours company.

Project Background

After working with PacSet Tours on my 2016 Web Design final, the 2017 Digital Marketing class offered an additional opportunity to continue working with them. The Spring season is the busiest for PacSet Tours, and as with the previous project, the interaction and direction from the client was limited for most of the semester.

Following the course syllabus, I researched, developed, and designed three hypothetical digital campaigns. All final documentation and research materials were submitted to PacSet Tours at the end of the semester.

My Role

Independently research market competition and target audience, develop a SWOT analysis and digital marketing proposal, and design three hypothetical digital campaigns.

Project Goals

  • Increase unique and repeated traffic to the site
  • Create consistent messaging in all social media channels
  • Form deeper relationships with current and past customers to develop brand amassadors
  • Create a schedule that supports the marketing lifecycle of a tour (the product) from inception to obsoletion

Technologies

  • Photoshop CC
  • Illustrator CC
  • InDesign CC
  • Clip Studio Paint
  • Mail Chimp
  • Microsoft PowerPoint

Market Research

Research began with a review of PacSet Tours social media practices, statistics, and a SWOT analysis. Additional research looked at other tour companies to find marketing opportunities that were overlooked or untapped by the competition. The comparison charted keywords, pricing, product itineraries, and target consumer personas.

Target Consumer Personas

Interviewing the CEO gave insight into the consumer personas that the company was pursuing. This information would be helpful for finding channels and opportunities to raise brand awareness.

Digital Campaign Proposal

The final Digital Marketing Campaign plan contained a messaging strategy, timeline, and estimated budget. The messaging strategy focused on utilizing social media, email newsletters, and the company blog to generate consistent and relevant content that supported the main message of "Travel Among Friends".

The estimated timeline and budget broadly scheduled advertising around the lifecycle of the tours and suggested non-digital channels as additional opportunities for raising brand awareness.

Hypothetical Digital Campaigns

Building off of the assets and independently designed branding from the 2016 Web Design final project, I created a sample newsletter, blog post, and modularly designed advertisement.
